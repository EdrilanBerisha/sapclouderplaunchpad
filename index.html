About

Why this extension?

This extension aims to improve your life as business user, developer, consultant or tester, working with different Fiori applications on different internal or customer systems.

Fast Navigation

Skip the slow launchpad loading. Open any Fiori App directly from your browser toolbar in seconds.

Favorites Management

Save your favorite SAP Systems and Fiori Apps. Open them in any system with a single click.

Team Collaboration

Export and share your system and app lists with colleagues or clients. Import backups when switching computers.

Incognito Support

Enable the extension in your browser's incognito mode to browse SAP systems privately.

Watch 3-min Overview on YouTube
Popup

Your Productivity Hub at a Click

Click the extension icon to open the popup and manage all your SAP systems and Fiori apps instantly.

Add Systems & Apps

Everything at your fingertips

By clicking on the icon, the popup opens and offers the possibility to add any Fiori App or SAP Cloud ERP System to your favorite list.

  • Add SAP Cloud ERP Systems with a single click
  • Bookmark any Fiori App as a "dynamic" shortcut
  • Navigate across systems without re-entering URLs
System Dropdown

Newly added systems appear instantly

Newly added SAP Cloud ERP Systems will be visible in the drop-down list on your popup. Switch between systems with ease.

Fiori App Favorites

Dynamic Fiori App bookmarks

Add a Fiori App to your favorite list and it will show up in your popup. This is basically a "dynamic" bookmark — open any Fiori app in any SAP Cloud ERP System.

  • System-agnostic app shortcuts
  • Combine favorite systems & apps
  • "Current System" option navigates within the active browser tab
Options

Full Control via the Options Page

Right-click the extension icon and select "Options" to configure your extension and manage your favorite lists.

Overview

Manage everything in one place

Configure your extension, maintain and manage your favorite lists, and share them with colleagues or clients.

Browser Settings

Open apps your way

Three segmented buttons — Current, Tab, Window — let you define how Fiori apps open:

  • Current — navigate in the current tab
  • Tab — open in a new browser tab
  • Window — open in a new browser window
Default System

Set your go-to system

Select the system you use the most as your default. It will always be pre-selected in the popup dropdown, saving you one more click every time.

Import / Export

Share & backup your lists

Use the upload and download functionality to share your favorite lists with colleagues or keep them as a backup when switching computers.

  • Export Fiori Apps or SAP Cloud ERP Systems list
  • Import lists from colleagues or backups
  • Pre-select the correct table tab before importing
Hidden Gem

Use it in Incognito Mode too

Want to use the extension in your incognito window? Enable it in your browser's extension settings — here's how:

1

Click the three dots, then navigate to Settings.

2

Navigate to the Extension settings in your browser.

3

Click on "Details" for the SAP Cloud ERP Launchpad extension.

4

Toggle the switch to enable the extension in incognito mode.
